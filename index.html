Neighborhood Studios · Annual General Meeting · 2026

Three years ago we started Neighborhood Studios because we believed there was a different way to build startups.

The opportunity was hiding in everyday life. Your neighborhood. Your home. Your kid’s school. The local business around the corner.

Our hunch was simple: if we could find real problems, validate sharp insights & test novel product wedges quickly, we could give great founders a meaningful head start.

The early results from Fund 1 are now in. We’ve learned a lot, built a lot, gotten some things wrong & seen enough to make us even more convinced than when we started.

So in November, we’re bringing everyone together for the first Neighborhood Studios Annual General Meeting for studio investors. We’ll share the companies, the numbers, the lessons & where we’re headed next with Fund 2.

We’ll dig in on Friday morning.

We’d love to have you there.
